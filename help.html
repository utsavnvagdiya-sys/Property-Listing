FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick Answers

How do I schedule a property viewing?
Click on any property listing and use the "Request a Viewing" form. Our agent will confirm a time within 24 hours. Viewings are available Monday–Sunday between 9am and 7pm.
What documents do I need to rent a property?
Typically you'll need a valid government ID or passport, proof of income (payslips or bank statements from the past 3 months), and references from a previous landlord or employer. We'll guide you through the full checklist once you've found a property.
How long does the buying process take?
The buying process typically takes 8–12 weeks from offer acceptance to completion, depending on mortgage approval, surveys and legal work. Our team will coordinate with solicitors and mortgage advisors to keep things moving efficiently.
Can I list my property for free?
Yes! You can add your property to our listings for free using the "Add Property" feature. Our team will review and publish your listing typically within 1 business day. Premium promotional slots are also available to boost visibility.
What areas do you cover?
We currently cover all major cities and suburbs across the United States, with over 50,000 active listings. We are continuously expanding our coverage — if your area isn't listed, contact us and we'll do our best to assist.
How are property prices determined?
Prices are set by the property owners and vetted by our agents. We regularly perform market comparisons to ensure listings are fairly priced. If you'd like a formal valuation, visit our Services page to book one with a certified valuer.
