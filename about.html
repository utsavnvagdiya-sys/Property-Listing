OUR STORY

Built on Trust & Excellence

Grandview Realty was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate property professionals who believed that finding a home should be an exciting, transparent, and empowering experience — not a stressful one.

What started as a small boutique agency with 5 agents has grown into one of the country's most trusted real estate platforms, with over 50,000 active listings, 10,000+ happy clients, and a team of 200+ dedicated property experts nationwide.

Our commitment to integrity, innovation, and exceptional service has earned us a 4.9/5 client satisfaction rating — and we're just getting started.
